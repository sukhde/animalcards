Giraffe

Giraffe's heart weighs about 25 pounds-it has to be that big to pump blood all the way to its brain.

  • Scientific Name:Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Average Length:4.2 m
  • Average Lifespan: 20-25 years
  • Habitat: Grassland

Giraffes are the world's tallest mammals, thanks to thier towering legs and long necks.A giraffe's legs alone are taller than many humans-about6 feet.The giraffe's stature can be a disadvantage as well-it is difficult and dangerous for a giraffe to drink at a water hole.
